Each writeup documents a complete assessment against a lab target — covering the full kill chain from initial reconnaissance through exploitation and privilege escalation, with a section on defensive considerations where relevant.

The goal is to record why each technique works, not just that it does. Methodology is consistent across all reports so they can be read as a body of work, not just isolated notes.
